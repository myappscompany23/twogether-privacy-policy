Privacy Policy for Twogether

Effective Date: 06.06.2025

Twogether ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (“App”). Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not require you to provide personally identifiable information to use the App. However, if you voluntarily provide us with information (e.g., through customer support), we may collect it.

b. Usage and Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you use the App, including but not limited to:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage data and interaction events

c. Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect and analyze usage patterns to improve the App's functionality and user experience. Firebase Analytics collects information such as events, user engagement, and device data.

d. Advertising Data

We display ads served by Google Ads within the App. Google Ads may collect data about your interaction with ads, device information, and other identifiers to deliver personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience.
  • Deliver personalized advertisements based on your interests.
  • Respond to customer support inquiries.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share data with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with service providers such as Firebase and Google Ads who help us operate and improve the App. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or through Google’s Ad Settings.
  • Disable Analytics: Some versions of the App may allow you to disable analytics tracking.
  • Access and Correction: Although we do not collect much personal data, if you request, we will provide access to any data we hold about you and correct inaccuracies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us to request deletion.

7. International Data Transfers

Since we operate globally, your data may be transferred and processed outside of your country of residence. We take steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
